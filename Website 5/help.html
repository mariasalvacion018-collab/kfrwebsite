Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Login/Register" in the top navigation, then select "Register here". Fill in your username, email, and password (minimum 6 characters) to create your account.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and look for a "Forgot password?" link. Enter your email and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I track my order?

After logging in, go to your profile page. Under "Your Orders" section, you'll see all your orders with their status and tracking information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards as well as cash on delivery for qualifying orders. Payment is processed securely at checkout.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery options may be available depending on your location and order size.

Can I cancel or modify an order?

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. For modifications, please cancel and reorder, or contact our support team immediately.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Contact support to start a return.

How do I update my profile?

Log in and go to your profile page. Click "Edit Profile" to change your username, email, or password. You'll need your current password to confirm changes.
